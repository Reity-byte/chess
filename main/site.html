Under the Hood

Negamax + Alpha-Beta

Depth-limited search with alpha-beta pruning cuts away branches that can't affect the result.

Iterative Deepening

The engine searches progressively deeper within its time budget, always keeping the best move found so far.

Quiescence Search

Capture sequences are played out past the search horizon to avoid misjudging tactical positions.

Transposition Table

Positions reached via different move orders are cached, so the engine never re-analyzes the same board twice.

Full Rule Support

  • Castling (kingside & queenside), en passant, and pawn promotion
  • Check, checkmate, and stalemate detection
  • Draws by threefold repetition, the 50-move rule, and insufficient material
